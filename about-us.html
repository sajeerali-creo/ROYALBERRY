Welcome to the Exquisite World of Royalberry & Cateriya by Intercat Hospitality

Step into a realm where heritage, tradition, & culinary artistry unite to create extraordinary experiences. Royalberry, the luxurious UAE extension of Purpleberry India, continues a legacy of excellence rooted in Indian culture, reimagined for the modern world. Together with Cateriya by Intercat Hospitality, we blend age-old traditions with contemporary innovation to redefine luxury catering in the Middle East & beyond.

From opulent weddings to exclusive corporate events, every occasion is a celebration of heritage & elegance. Our dishes, inspired by the rich flavors of Indian & international cuisines, embody the perfect harmony of tradition & fusion. With bespoke service, tailored experiences, & meticulous attention to detail, Royalberry & Cateriya deliver moments that transcend expectations & leave lasting impressions.

Our Mission

At Royalberry, we believe that every event is a story waiting to be told. Our mission is to transform your vision into a masterpiece through flawless execution & personalized service. Each setup, inspired by heritage themes & contemporary elegance, is thoughtfully designed to honor cultural traditions while enhancing the beauty of your chosen venue. Whether it's a destination wedding, a corporate gala, or an intimate gathering, we craft memorable experiences that reflect your unique style, ensuring that every detail is as meaningful as the moments they create.

Our Vision

We aspire to be the global leaders in luxury catering & hospitality, specializing in destination weddings, celebrations, artisanal mithai, heritage-inspired industrial catering, fine dining, & cloud kitchens. With a recent merger with Cateriya, we combine decades of expertise to deliver unparalleled experiences that merge tradition & innovation. Our commitment to quality, cultural authenticity, & competitive pricing ensures that every event is elevated with elegance & sophistication, leaving an indelible mark on every guest.
